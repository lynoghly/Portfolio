Filmmaker

Welcome to my professional portfolio. I'm Lyn, a dedicated filmmaker with a passion for directing and editing films and documentaries. Currently pursuing Film and Screen Studies with a minor in Journalism and Digital Storytelling, I focus on bringing compelling narratives to life.

My projects revolve around creating films, developing a prompt into a captivating film, and exploring documentary storytelling. With a strong foundation in cinematography and editing, I aim to deliver visual experiences that are authentic, thought-provoking, and impactful.

Committed to continuous learning and collaboration, I actively seek opportunities to expand my creative horizons and refine my craft. My goal is to tell stories that resonate with audiences, spark meaningful conversations, and inspire change.
